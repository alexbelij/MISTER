Footage & Note Analysis

Analyze match footage with on-device VLM, OCR handwritten notes, translate briefings for multinational squads.

🎬 Footage Analysis (QVAC VLA + Video)

Upload a match frame or video clip. The VLM analyzes formation, pressing, space, and tactical patterns. Results are interpreted through your club's game model.

📝 OCR Handwritten Notes (QVAC OCR)

Photograph the coach's handwritten match notes. OCR extracts text, structures it (match/player/training/tactical), and converts to SFT pairs for training.

🌍 Tactical Translator (QVAC Translate)

Translate match briefings into 16+ languages for multinational squads. EM clubs with legioners get instructions in their native language — all on-device.
